# 🚀 BizPilot AI

> Your intelligent AI business assistant for smarter decisions, streamlined operations, and accelerated growth.

BizPilot AI helps businesses automate workflows, analyze information, and make better decisions using the power of artificial intelligence.

Built with modern web technologies, BizPilot AI is designed to help entrepreneurs, teams, and organizations save time, increase productivity, and operate more efficiently.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🤖 AI Business Assistant
Interact with an intelligent AI system that helps you generate insights, answer business questions, and automate repetitive tasks.

### 📊 Smart Analytics & Insights
Turn business data into actionable recommendations with AI-powered analysis.

### ⚡ Workflow Automation
Reduce manual work by automating everyday business processes.

### 🧠 Decision Support
Get AI-assisted recommendations to help plan strategies, identify opportunities, and solve challenges.

### 🔐 Secure & Scalable
Built with modern technologies to support reliable performance and future growth.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

BizPilot AI is built using:

- **Next.js** – React framework for fast, scalable applications
- **TypeScript** – Reliable and maintainable development
- **Tailwind CSS** – Modern responsive styling
- **AI APIs** – Intelligent automation and assistance
- **Vercel** – Deployment and hosting platform
