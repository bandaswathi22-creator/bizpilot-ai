# 🚀 BizPilot AI

> Your AI Workforce for Modern Business

BizPilot AI is a multi-agent AI business assistant that helps entrepreneurs and teams make smarter decisions, automate workflows, and improve business operations.

Built for the **Kanz AI Training Hackathon**, BizPilot AI provides specialized AI agents for sales, finance, marketing, analytics, documents, customer support, and business strategy.

---

# 🎯 Problem

Small businesses often struggle with:

- Creating effective sales strategies
- Understanding financial health
- Planning marketing campaigns
- Analyzing business performance
- Managing customer support
- Creating professional business documents

Business owners need simple AI tools that provide practical recommendations.

---

# 💡 Solution

BizPilot AI creates a virtual AI workforce where each AI agent specializes in a different business function.

Users can ask business questions and receive actionable recommendations instantly.
